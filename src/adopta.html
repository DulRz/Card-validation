Have you ever thought about adopting a pet? What has stopped you?

Perhaps the desire has always been there, but for some reason, you think it isn't the right time. This is more common than you think and it's because, to take the next step, it's essential to establish a real and physical connection with an animal and go beyond the idea of adoption. Visiting an animal shelter is a good first step in this process.
In PetsFriends Wasi you'll find many four-legged friends in need of lots of love, eager to become part of your family. If you wish to ADOPT one of our furry friends, you can fill out the following form, and within a period of 72 hours, we'll be in touch with you to coordinate an interview. It's very important to be 18 years or older, and if you are underage, you can ask your parents to complete the form.

Thank you for being part of our family!
